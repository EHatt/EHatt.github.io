Scope it out - An Adventure into Space

Astronomy relies on our ability to look into space and gather data on the objects we see. Picking the right tool for the job is essential, not only to ensure the observations you make are useful, but also to convince other scientists that your idea is worth the time! Follow the instructions you have been given, using this page to guide your decisions. Once you have finished all the steps, submit your choices at the bottom of the page and discover the results of your mission!
