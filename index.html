Scope it out - An Adventure into Space

Astronomy relies on our ability to look into space and gather data on the objects we see. Picking the right tool for the job is essential, not only to ensure the observations you make are useful, but also to convince other scientists that your idea is worth the time!

Download the instructions for this session using the 'instructions' button below . To keep track of the choices you make you will also need the proposal template, which you can download using the 'proposal template' button . Once you have finished all the steps, you'll be given another link to submit your proposal!
