Astronomy Outreach

Welcome to the Astronomy workshop, everything you need for today's session can be found using this website. After listening to a couple of talks from some of our researchers, you'll have two activities to participate in. During the first you'll get to design your first proposal for an observing mission. For this, follow the link to mission proposal. The second activity will guide you through how we calculate which planets would be able to host life. For this, follow the link to habitability.
